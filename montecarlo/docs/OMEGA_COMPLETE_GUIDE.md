## 🧮 Risk Metrics & Physics-Based Analysis

### Traditional Risk Metrics
- **Value at Risk (VaR)**: 95% confidence loss estimate
- **Conditional VaR (CVaR)**: Tail risk (worst 5%)
- **Sharpe Ratio**: Risk-adjusted returns
- **Sortino Ratio**: Downside risk adjusted
- **Maximum Drawdown**: Peak-to-trough decline

### GenesiX Physics-Based Metrics
- **Hurst Exponent**: Market momentum (>0.5) vs mean-reversion (<0.5)
- **Market Temperature**: Volatility & stress level (0-100°)
- **Tail Index (Alpha)**: Power law fit for extreme events
- **Criticality Index**: Phase transition detection
- **Percolation R₀**: Contagion probability

## 🔄 Rebalancing Engine

### Rebalancing Frequencies
- **Monthly**: For active traders (highest transaction costs)
- **Quarterly**: Recommended for most investors
- **Semi-Annual**: For passive investors
- **Annual**: Tax-efficient, minimal monitoring

### Drift Thresholds
- ±5% drift triggers rebalancing recommendation
- ±10% drift triggers automatic alert
- Single position > target*1.5 = reduce

### Benefits
- Maintains target risk exposure
- Forces "sell high, buy low" discipline
- Can generate tax-loss harvesting opportunities
